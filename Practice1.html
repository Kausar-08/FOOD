EFO RIRO

`

Create a comforting soup by boiling efo tete with assorted stocks and spices. Blend
tatashe,tomatoes and atarodo for a fragrant paste,then simmer it with spices,
locust beans and efo tete. Savor this Nigerian-inspired dish for a hearty and nutritious meal
thats both easy to make and delicious

INGREDIENTS

1. Efo tete

2. Palm oil

3. Ata rodo

4. Tatashe

5. Ponmo

6. Any stock of your choice

7. Locust bean

8. Smoked fish

9. Crayfish (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Boil your stocks,resere the water and fry them if necessary

2. Blend tatashe,ata rodo,onions and tomato

3. Add the palm oil in the pot,fry the onions and iru till translucent

4. Then add your blended paste to the oil

5. Add your seasonings and stir to taste

6. Then add your stocks and assorted and stir together

7. Allow it to fry for 10minutes

8. Then add your neatly washed efo tete

9. Allow to cook for 15minutes

10. Then stir till perfection

11. Your efo riro is ready and ready to be served with any swallow of your choice.
